Introducing Scribe

Scribe is an iOS application that enables the creation and seamless integration of relevant content into the podcast experience.

View Prototype
About Scribe

The current podcast listening experience is not optimized for learning and interactivity. Podcasts are passive and audio-only which does not meet the needs of active listeners who want more from the podcast experience. Current platforms lack the ability for listeners to engage with podcast content and relevant information.

With Scribe, listeners can now engage with podcasts in ways they never have before. Scribe creates a more dynamic and engaging experience that enhances podcast content and empowers active listeners to extract and retain knowledge.

Features

The Scribe Difference

Podcast creators can curate relevant supplemental content to engage listeners including links & key timestamps.

Podcast creators can highlight advertisers to their audience.

Listeners can save & store notes provided by podcast creators.

Listeners can enhance their experience with the ability to add notes at specific points in the podcast.

The ability for listeners to share their notes with others.

Listeners can save important podcast points on the go through their Apple Watch.
